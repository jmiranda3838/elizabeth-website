Therapy Is For Everyone

We all have concerns, questions, and troubles—difficult or confusing times. As your therapist, I provide a safe space for us to explore solutions to life's challenges... together.

In my practice, you will be received with warmth, kindness, and genuine concern. I view problems as separate from people—whatever the issue may be, I strive to understand it through your lens.

Using strengths-based, narrative, and mindfulness methods, we will explore your skills, abilities, ideals, and beliefs so you have a rich understanding of what is meaningful to you.

Sacred Promises

No Judgment

I will NOT judge you. This is a sanctuary free from criticism.

Unconditional Respect

I will RESPECT and HONOR your thoughts, feelings, opinions, and ideas unconditionally.

You Are The Expert

I will not tell you what to do. I believe you are the EXPERT on your own life.

Facilitator of Growth

I will guide you as you explore what you VALUE—and how to approach your life in ways that reflect those values.

Your Journey

Therapy is all about YOU. I will be open, honest, genuine, and sincere—still, therapy is about YOUR journey.

Your Hidden Strength

No problem is bigger than your strength

Beneath every struggle lies a root system of resilience you may have forgotten. We will work collaboratively as I guide you—exploring what troubles you and how problems influence your life, decisions, and relationships.

I will support you as we work together to find relief from whatever weighs on you, so you may live a lighter, brighter, and more joyful life.
