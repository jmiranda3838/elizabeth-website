My Approach

Narrative therapy starts with your story

I believe you are the expert on your own life. My role isn't to diagnose or fix, but to walk alongside you with curiosity and care. Narrative therapy helps us examine the stories you've been told, the ones you tell yourself, and the ones that haven't yet found voice.

Together, we'll separate you from the problem, discover the values that guide you, and uncover the "sparkling moments" that point toward your preferred way of living.

Collaborative

We work together as partners. You set the direction; I offer reflection and support.

Respectful & Non-Pathologizing

Problems are problems; you are not the problem. We work with struggles, not labels.

Culturally Aware

I honor the cultural, social, and personal contexts that shape your experience.

Practical & Reflective

We balance deep reflection with real-world strategies you can use today.

How It Works

Starting therapy is simple

Free Consultation

New Moon

We'll have a brief call to discuss what brings you to therapy and answer any questions. This helps us both determine if we're a good fit.

First Session

Waxing Moon

In our first meeting, we'll explore your story more deeply. I'll get to know you and what matters most. You'll get a sense of how we work together.

Ongoing Work

Full Moon

We'll meet regularly (typically weekly) to continue our exploration. Each session builds on the last, moving at a pace that feels right for you.

What Clients Often Tell Me

When people reach out, they often share thoughts like these...

I want to feel like myself again, but I'm not even sure who that is anymore.

I know what I should do, but I can't seem to make it happen.

I want to stop having the same argument over and over with my partner.

I need a space where I don't have to perform or pretend everything is fine.

I want to understand why I keep getting in my own way.

If any of these resonate, you're in the right place.
